About Us

Equatorial Medical Company LTD Since 2021

Founded in Khartoum and now headquartered in El Gedaref State, Equatorial Medical Company LTD is dedicated to transforming healthcare access across Sudan. We bridge critical gaps in the supply chain, ensuring even the most underserved communities receive the highest quality medical products and services.

  • Headquarters: El Gedaref State, Sudan
  • Team: Multidisciplinary professionals in healthcare, logistics, and regulatory affairs
  • Network: Nationwide operations, collaborating with KOLs, healthcare professionals, and distribution partners
